Katrien Antonio

professor in actuarial science and insurance analytics

KU Leuven

University of Amsterdam

I am a professor in actuarial science and insurance analytics at KU Leuven and (part-time) professor in actuarial data science with the University of Amsterdam in the Netherlands. I love teaching at all levels of higher education and enjoy creating engaging materials (e.g. videos, presentations, research papers) for sharing data-driven insights.

Interests

  • life, non-life and health insurance
  • insurance analytics
  • statistical learning
  • machine learning
